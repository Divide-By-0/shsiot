Purpose

May 27, 2017May 28, 2017

Come to club meetings in Pickett Room 107 on Mondays during lunch to learn how to work with sensors and their data, and gain experience to intern at massive companies like Google, Apple, and Intel!

At our meetings, we will work with various sensors and boards such as Arduino and Raspberry Pi, and work together on automation projects to improve our school and homes. In the process, members of all skill levels can learn how to code, and integrate it with the hardware provided.

We build projects that are actually fun to use and provide an immersive learning experience. For more precise meeting info, make sure to join our facebook group at https://www.facebook.com/groups/1501573740155452/.
